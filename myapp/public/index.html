Welcome to our project!

Our team has developed a responsive web application that allows users to track their heart rate and blood oxygen values. We used the Particle Cloud platform to connect our sensors and collect data, and utilized webhooks to send that data to our server. We then used Amazon Web Services to store and manage the data, allowing our users to access it from any device with an internet connection. With our application, users can easily monitor their heart health and make necessary lifestyle changes to improve their overall well-being.
